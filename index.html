About Us

Welcome to WORLD WILDLIFE FUND, Advocates for Wildlife and Nature Conservation!

Our Mission:

At WORLD WILDLIFE FUND, we aim to:

Educate our community about the importance of conserving nature. Engage individuals in meaningful actions to support environmental sustainability. Empower everyone to contribute to WWF's conservation efforts through knowledge, volunteerism, and donations. Join us in making a difference. Explore, learn, and become part of a movement dedicated to creating a future where humans live in harmony with nature.

Together, we can protect our planet's precious wildlife and habitats.

Members

Carter Roberts

President and CEO

WWF’s strength is in its global network of passionate individuals who are committed to protecting our natural world. Its members, from dedicated conservationists and scientists to concerned citizens and philanthropists, form the backbone of our organization. They work tirelessly across nearly 100 countries, contributing to over 3,000 projects aimed at preserving wildlife, restoring habitats, and fostering sustainable practices. By joining WWF, you become part of a community that values the conservation of nature and the myriad of life it sustains. Every member’s effort, whether big or small, creates ripples that extend far beyond their immediate environment, inspiring others to take action for our planet’s well-being.
